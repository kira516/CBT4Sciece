About CBT4Science

Who We Are

CBT4Science is an online learning platform designed
to help students prepare for JAMB and other computer-based
tests through engaging and realistic CBT practice.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple - to make exam preparation smart,
simple, and accessible for every student, anytime and anyhere.

What We Offer

Practice questions based on JAMB syllabus.
Instant feedback and detailed results.
Timed CBT exams for speed training.
Performance tracking and analytics.
Study tips and learning resources.

Why Choose Us

CBT4Science combines simplicity with powerfull learning tool.
Our platform give you the best exam experience and help you
track your progress until you achieve success.

Keep practicing - success is one click away!
